About Us

T S Logistic Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2012 and has been dedicated to providing reliable roadways transportation services ever since. Our company is led by two experienced directors, Mr. Kapil Sharma and Mr. Ankit Sharma, who each bring over 20+ years of expertise in the logistics industry. We focus exclusively on road transport, ensuring that our clients receive efficient and dependable services for their transportation needs. Our commitment to straightforward and effective solutions makes us a trusted partner for businesses looking to manage their logistics smoothly. At T S Logistic Solutions, our goal is to deliver consistent and reliable road transport services that meet the specific needs of our clients.
